Introduction

Here at ClickSnap Photography we are dedicated in helping people to grow and discover their passion for photography. We offer a wide range of classes for photographers of all skill levels and experiences. Whether you’re here to gain a new skill, start a new hobby, kick start a new career or to gain new knowledge and a better understanding about photography; everyone is welcome.

Our classes provide hands on teaching through practical demonstrations at a pace you’re comfortable with. From beginner to advanced, each course is available to start at your chosen skill level.

About

We have provided a list of the courses we offer; covering a range of techniques see below what each entails:

Photographing nature at its finest, landscape photography is about capturing the beauty of scenic environments.

Taking extreme close up photographs of very small subjects such as insects or plants. As a result, this makes them look much larger which enables you to capture every intricate detail.

A wide angled photograph usually taken of a landscape scene. This can also be done by capturing multiple shots at different angles, which are then edited together to create one long image.

Photographing a person or group of people and primarily focusing on their facial features and expressions in order to capture the personality of the subject.

By controlling the length of time the shutter stays open, you can create effects such as freezing or blurring motion. This happens due to the exposure of light hitting the camera sensor.

This effect is created when a subject is placed in front of a strongly lit background, when the photograph is taken the subject will be seen without any detail as a blacked-out shape forming a silhouette.

Photographing non moving objects such as flowers, food or plants. It is important to make good use of the setting, focusing on lighting and placement.
